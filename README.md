# Sonic DE Branding Guidelines

## Conditions of usage

1. The term "Sonic DE logo" refers to the Sonic DE logo and other official artwork/mark. It also includes the official color scheme used by the project.

2. The term "Sonic DE library" refers to the Sonic DE tooling, associated libraries and official projects in the Sonic DE ecosystem.

3. Usage of the Sonic DE logo must not give the impression or implication that the KDE Lite project (or any contributor to the project) is sponsoring or endorsing any other project, service, product or organization.

4. Usage of the Sonic DE logo, to indicate, imply or assert compatibility or operability with the Sonic DE library, must be accurate and done in good faith.
