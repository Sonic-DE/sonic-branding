# KDE Lite Branding Guidelines

## Conditions of usage

1. The term "KDE Lite logo" refers to the KDE Lite logo and other official artwork/mark. It also includes the official color scheme used by the project.

2. The term "KDE Lite library" refers to the KDE Lite tooling, associated libraries and official projects in the KDE Lite ecosystem.

3. Usage of the KDE Lite logo must not give the impression or implication that the KDE Lite project (or any contributor to the project) is sponsoring or endorsing any other project, service, product or organization.

4. Usage of the KDE Lite logo, to indicate, imply or assert compatibility or operability with the KDE Lite library, must be accurate and done in good faith.
